Frequently Asked Questions (Mailbox Locks)

What should I do if I lost my HDB letterbox key?
If you've lost your HDB letterbox key, don't worry. Contact our 24/7 emergency service. We can either unlock your existing letterbox for a SGD 25 fee, or we can replace the entire lock with a new digital one, which includes the unlocking service. Our prices for changing a mailbox lock start from just SGD 50. This price includes the new lock and the full installation service. If your current lock is locked and you've lost the key, an additional SGD 25 unlocking fee applies.

Our most popular password and key models (Product 1, 2, 3, 6, 7) are fully mechanical and do not require batteries. Only the "Smart Battery-Powered" locks (Product 4, 8) use CR2032 batteries, which last over 12 months.
What is "Retrieve Password" on some locks?
Our models like Product 2, 3, 6, and 7 have a password retrieval mechanism. This means if you forget your code, you don't need to break the lock. We can use a master key or tool to find your password for you. Crucially, this key cannot open the lock directly.

Yes, absolutely! Our service is primarily focused on replacing old, faulty, or key-based locks with modern digital and smart options. Our team ensures a perfect fit for nearly all HDB and condominium letterbox models.

Mechanical digital locks are highly secure. Especially our Product 6 (4-Digit Lock), which offers thousands more combinations than standard 3-digit locks, making it extremely secure against guessing.
