If you've lost your HDB letterbox key, don't worry. Contact our 24/7 emergency service. Do your digital mailbox locks require batteries?
Our most popular password and key models (Product 1, 2, 3, 6, 8, 9) are fully mechanical and do not require batteries. Only the "Smart Battery-Powered" locks (Product 4, 7) use CR2032 batteries, which last over 12 months.
What is "Retrieve Password" on some locks?
Our models like Product 3, 5, 6, 8 and 9 have a password retrieval mechanism. This means if you forget your code, you don't need to break the lock. We can use a master key or tool to find your password for you. Crucially, this key cannot open the lock directly.

Yes, absolutely! Our service is primarily focused on replacing old, faulty, or key-based locks with modern digital and smart options. Our team ensures a perfect fit for nearly all HDB and condominium letterbox models.

Mechanical digital locks are highly secure. Especially our Product 8 & 9 (4-Digit Locks), which offer 10,000 combinations, making them extremely secure against guessing.
