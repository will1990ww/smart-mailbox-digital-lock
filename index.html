Frequently Asked Questions (Mailbox Locks)

What should I do if I lost my HDB letterbox key?
Don't panic! Contact our emergency locksmith service. We can professionally pick and unlock your existing letterbox for a nominal $15 fee. We will then immediately replace it with a secure digital letterbox lock, so you never have to worry about losing small keys again.
How much does it cost to change a letterbox lock in Singapore?
Our premium keyless lock installations start from SGD 70. This nett price includes the brand new digital lock, transport, and our professional installation labor. If your box is currently locked and requires picking, the total will just be the lock price plus the $15 unlock fee.
Is it legal to change my HDB or Condo letterbox lock?
Yes, absolutely. While the master door is accessed by the postman, the individual lock mechanism for your specific unit belongs to the homeowner. Upgrading to a digital or smart letterbox lock is completely legal and a widely accepted security upgrade in Singapore.
Are your digital locks compatible with standard Singpost mailboxes?
Yes. Our digital and mechanical models are precision-engineered to be direct replacements for the standard Singpost cam locks. They fit perfectly into 99% of Singapore's HDB and Condominium letterbox doors without requiring any drilling or permanent modification.
What happens if the battery dies on a Smart Mailbox Lock?
Most of our popular locks (P2, P3, P5, P6, P8, P9) are fully mechanical and do not require batteries. For our Smart Battery Locks (P4, P7), they run on a high-capacity CR2032 battery that lasts over 12 months. The lock will provide a low-battery warning. Some models also feature an external power backup or mechanical override key for emergencies.
Are these digital locks anti-theft and secure?
Yes, they provide superior security compared to standard thin keys. Our 4-digit models (P8, P9) offer 10,000 possible combinations, making them highly resistant to guessing. The heavy-duty Zinc Alloy construction (P6, P9) also acts as a strong physical anti-theft deterrent.
Will the installation or replacement damage my letterbox?
No. We utilize specialized locksmithing equipment and techniques to ensure zero structural damage, scratches, or dents to your letterbox door during the upgrade or unlocking process.
