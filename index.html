Frequently Asked Questions (Mailbox Locks)

What should I do if I lost my HDB letterbox key?
If you've lost your HDB letterbox key, don't worry. Contact our 24/7 emergency service. We can either unlock your existing letterbox for a SGD 25 fee, or we can replace the entire lock with a new digital one.
Will you damage my letterbox during installation?
No. Our technicians are professional locksmiths who use specialized tools to ensure a clean installation or replacement with no damage to your letterbox door.
How much does it cost to change a mailbox lock?
Our prices start from just SGD 50 for a traditional key lock or battery-free mechanical lock. This includes installation. Unlocking fees apply if the mailbox is currently locked.
What is the "Retrieve Password" function?
Models like Product 2, 3, 5, 6, and 8 feature a password retrieval mechanism. If you forget your code, we can use a special key to find your password without breaking the lock. Note: This key cannot open the lock directly, only retrieve the code.
How fast can you install?
We usually arrange installation within 1-2 business days. Installation typically takes about 15-20 minutes.
Do your digital mailbox locks require batteries?
Our mechanical models (Prod 2, 3, 5, 6, 8) do NOT require batteries. Our smart models (Prod 4, 7) use CR2032 batteries which last over 12 months.
Is there a warranty?
Yes! All our digital and mechanical mailbox locks include a **1-Year Seller Warranty with on-call support** ‚Äì giving you complete peace of mind.
