Frequently Asked Questions (Mailbox Locks)

What should I do if I lost my HDB letterbox key?
If you've lost your HDB letterbox key, don't worry. Contact our 24/7 emergency service. We can either unlock your existing letterbox for a SGD 25 fee, or we can replace the entire lock with a new digital one.
How much does it cost to change a mailbox lock?
Our prices start from just SGD 50 for a traditional key lock or battery-free mechanical lock. This includes installation.
How fast can you install?
We usually arrange installation within 1-2 business days. Installation takes about 20 minutes.
Is there a warranty?
Yes! All our digital and mechanical mailbox locks include a **1-Year Seller Warranty with on-call support**.
Do your digital mailbox locks require batteries?
Mechanical locks (P2, P3, P5, P6, P8) do NOT require batteries. Smart locks (P4, P7) use CR2032 batteries which last over 12 months.
What is the "Retrieve Password" function?
Models like P3, P5, P6, and P8 feature a key that can find your password if you forget it. Note: This key is for retrieval only, it cannot unlock the mailbox directly.
Will you damage my letterbox?
No. Our technicians are professional locksmiths who use specialized tools to ensure a clean installation with no damage to your door.
