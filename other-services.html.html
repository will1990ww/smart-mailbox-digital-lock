Our General Locksmith Services

We are a full-service locksmith company offering a wide range of solutions. Our professional technicians can handle all types of lock installations, replacements, and repairs.

  • Door Lock Services: We can replace, repair, or install new locks for HDB room doors, condo bedroom doors, and office doors (non-main door).
  • General Lock Repair: If you have a lock that is spoiled, jammed, or difficult to use, we can diagnose the problem and provide a fast, affordable repair.

  • 24/7 Emergency Unlock Service: Locked out of your room, cabinet, or drawer? Our 24/7 emergency team can assist with non-destructive unlocking services.
